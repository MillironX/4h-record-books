I seen you before. Hazel cut your pic'ure out of the paper. Kenny in The Home Ranch by Ralph Moody

Requirements

Juniors (Ages 8-10) Intermediates (Ages 11-13) Seniors (Ages 14+)
Length 1 page* 2 pages*
Content Only photographs and newspaper clippings - No ribbons, certificates, etc.
Record Every project must be addressed
Labeling Every photograph must be clearly labeled with a date and descriptive caption
Arrangement Photographs and clippings may not overlap
* These pages may be double-sided if plastic page protectors are used

Use this section of your record book to use pictures to tell about your 4-H projects and year. To make this section more engaging, include photos from the beginning, middle, and end of each project. Oh, and use action shots: they are much more fun to look at and tell and better story. If you have a particular part of your record book that you found hard to describe in words, include a picture here to clarify.
